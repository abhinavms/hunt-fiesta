About IEEE SCT SB

IEEE started off in Sree Chitra Thirunal College Of Engineering  with the registration of members on 23rd October 2009 with 30 members in it's first phase. The Student Branch is one of the top 10 SBs in the Travancore Hub. Sree Chitra Thirunal College Of Engineering is one of the leading engineering colleges in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala.
