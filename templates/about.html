Event Guidelines

Rules for answers

Your answer must be in all small letter.

No symbols, space or capital letter is accepted as answers.

Example : If there are two words like Captain America. Then your answer should be captainamerica.

Your answer can also be numbers.

General Rules

Sharing of answers and any other malpractices are strictly prohibited and if found will lead to disqualification.
